Social Activity

Working for Better Student and Rural Health, Education and Culture

He is a social activist and working hard in health care for rural poor students. Dr Chowdhury is an excellent social worker. He is the founder Chairman of a self-fund, non-partisan organization “General Health Care For Rural Student”. Dr Chowdhury and his health care team provided free health care to thousands of poor rural students and their family members.

Dr Chowdhury encourages the young doctors to learn surgery by presenting the critical surgical cases in the morning scientific session of the department.

Dr Chowdhury is receiving Crest of Honour for contribution in Rural Education

Dr Chowdhury is receiving crest of honour from UNO Mrs Dilshat Begum for excellent social and cultural work in Boalkhali Upazila
