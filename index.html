About

Dr Ashish Kumar Chowdhury is skilled in the surgery of GI Tract, Biliary Tract, Rectum, Anal Canal and Colon, Hernia, Breast, Urinary System, varicose veins of lower limb, salivary gland, thyroid gland, prostate, external genitalia. He is also expertise in basic plastic and reconstructive surgery and laparoscopic surgery.
